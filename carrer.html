Defamation and False Claims

1. Prohibition of Defamation

Cyfer Code strictly prohibits any individual or entity from making false, misleading, or defamatory statements about the company, its services, employees, affiliates, or business practices. Defamation includes any communication, whether written, oral, or through any other medium, that harms the reputation of Cyfer Code by presenting false information as fact.

2. Legal Consequences of Defamation

Any individual or entity found to have made defamatory statements about Cyfer Code may face legal consequences. Cyfer Code reserves the right to pursue claims for defamation, including seeking damages for harm caused to its reputation, loss of business, or other related damages. Legal remedies may include monetary compensation, retractions, and other appropriate relief as permitted by law.

3. Reporting and Response

If Cyfer Code becomes aware of any defamatory statements or false claims made about the company, it will take immediate action to investigate and address the issue. This may include issuing legal notices, pursuing legal action, and seeking public retractions from the offending party.

4. Protection of Reputation

Cyfer Code is committed to protecting its reputation and the integrity of its business. The company will take all necessary legal steps to defend against defamation and ensure that false or misleading statements are corrected or removed.

5. No Tolerance for False Claims

Cyfer Code does not tolerate the dissemination of false claims regarding its services, products, or business practices. Any individual or entity making such claims without factual basis or with the intent to harm the company may be subject to legal action, including defamation claims and other appropriate measures to protect the company’s reputation and business interests.
